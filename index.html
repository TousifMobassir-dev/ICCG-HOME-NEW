About Us

THE ISLAMIC CENTER OF CAPE GIRARDEAU (ICCG) IS A RELIGIOUS AND COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION LOCATED IN CAPE GIRARDEAU, MISSOURI, UNITED STATES.

Our Beliefs?

We believe that God is Divine, Just, Ever-Forgiving, Merciful and Most Gracious. Our beliefs reflect the sacred thoughts of major historic religious doctrines. We respect and love all of God’s prophets and messengers, starting from Adam and ending with the Prophet Mohammed (Peace ).

Mission Statement?

Islamic Center of Cape Girardeau is a non-profit, Islamic organization recognized as tax-exempt entity under U.S. Internal Revenue Code section 501(c)(3). The objective of ICCG is to develop, support and promote an Islamic way of life and to ensure the emergence of an American Muslim identity.

Committees?

ICCG vision consists of not only being a model community for its community members, but also for the American Muslim community and society-at-large.

Ladies of the ICCG?

The Ladies of the ICCG is a group that works to support the mission of the Islamic Center of Cape Girardeau. Building bridges with other community organizations, they focus on assisting, empowering, and raising awareness on issues within our community.
